Mag-KAAGAPAY ta bai! kawsa sa agrikultura ug panginabuhian sa barangay

DAVNOR KAAGAPAY is the flagship program of the provincial government of Davao del Norte thru the initiative of the Governor, as he is driven to extenuate the Food Security of his constituents.

The program started during the start of the 2020 Corona Virus Pandemic and has continued to yield affirmative results for the past two years.
